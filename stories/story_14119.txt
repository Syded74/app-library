A/N and all-purpose disclaimer: Written for reel_merlin, for the movie prompt of the same name. If you've seen the movie, you'll recognize the bits I took from it. I also lifted a few select lines from the show. Also, I blatantly stole a bunch of other stuff, which I will list below:

-All but one of the Demotivational posters that Arthur and Merlin use to flirt harass each other are from despair.com, because they're hilarious. (The one containing the line "Vanish from my sight, peon" is from rageprufrock's Three Castles, and had to be used because it is one of my favorite fanfic lines in the history of ever.)

-The vegetarian joke mug is copyrighted as well, but I have no idea where I got it from.

-Silver spotted skippers are a real endangered butterfly in Britain, and I thought the name was hilarious so I used them. Sadly, they do not actually feed on the Mortius flower.

-The butterflies became an unexpected symbol/metaphor/allegory for Merlin and Arthur and how their relationship changes them both. I really can't quite figure out how that ended up happening, because I just look at this and think 'Ahahaha! I can't believe I actually tried to insert something deep and </i>symbolic</i> into this mindless drivel!'

-The 12-foot penis flower is a real endangered plant. As soon as I read about it, I knew I had to mention it in this fic.

-There is a very tiny allusion to syllic's WIP Easy There. See if you can spot it.

-Also, I'm afraid I have Merlin channeling Ianto for the coffee thing (though, honestly, that could also just be me projecting my coffee!love onto Merlin). I don't know how that happened. I don't read Torchwood fic.
